THE FOUNTAIN



Step back in time and enjoy the nostalgia of our old-fashion soda fountain. Days gone by are captured when you order up old time favorites, like cherry Cokes, milkshakes made with hand-dipped ice cream or hand patted hamburgers. Whether you are hungry for a hearty breakfast, fast and enjoyable lunch or early afternoon snack, The Fountain offers a wide range of delicious choices. Order off the menu or try one of our Daily Specials. Great food served at a great price. That’s the reason that after 60 years, you’ll still hear people say, “Meet me at Smith’s Drugs Fountain”.



Breakfast is served from 7:30 AM -10:30 AM (11:30 on Saturday)
Lunch until 3PM

Directions

Directions from Caroleen/Ellenboro/Shelby

Head west on Hwy 74. Take the Forest City/Alexander Mills exit. Circle around on exit and turn left onto Broadway St. Follow Broadway St. until it T-stops at Kimbrell’s Furniture and stop light. Turn left onto Main St. Smiths’ Drugs of Forest City is two blocks down on the right.

Directions from Rutherfordton/Spindale

Head east on Hwy 74. Take the Forest City/Alexander Mills exit. At top of ramp, turn left onto Broadway St. Follow Broadway St. until it T-stops at Kimbrell’s Furniture and stop light. Turn left onto Main St. Smith’s Drugs of Forest City is two blocks down on the right.
