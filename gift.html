The Gift Department


If you like the cozy feel of cottage living or the style of days-gone-by, then a visit to our gift department will be pure pleasure! We have a selection of nostalgic home décor items, traditional-themed accents and special interest wall ornaments and adornments. Seasonal decorating is a snap as we feature each holiday and time of year with changing items. Stroll through, browse and enjoy new treasures with every visit. Our children’s section boasts a selection of old-fashioned toys, over-sized plush animals and games.

Visit and see our assortment of accessories that will make anyone a fashion hit. Purses in clutch and over-the shoulder styles, bracelets of single and multiple strands, matching earring/necklace sets, retro designs and genuine stones. Whether you like large, statement-making jewelry or a delicate addition of color to an outfit, we’ll have something to fit your style.

Directions from Caroleen/Ellenboro/Shelby

Head west on Hwy 74. Take the Forest City/Alexander Mills exit. Circle around on exit and turn left onto Broadway St. Follow Broadway St. until it T-stops at Kimbrell’s Furniture and stop light. Turn left onto Main St. Smiths’ Drugs of Forest City is two blocks down on the right.

Directions from Rutherfordton/Spindale

Head east on Hwy 74. Take the Forest City/Alexander Mills exit. At top of ramp, turn left onto Broadway St. Follow Broadway St. until it T-stops at Kimbrell’s Furniture and stop light. Turn left onto Main St. Smith’s Drugs of Forest City is two blocks down on the right.
